Project Highlights

Market Segment Booking Pace - Summary Analysis

  • Analyzed booking trends across Direct and OTA channels from 2018 to 2020 using historical OTB data.
  • Segmented bookings by lead time (0–7, 8–14, 15–30, 31+ days) to assess booking window behaviors.
  • Grouped results by weekly arrival date to track pace over time.
  • Created filters by hotel property and distribution channel for dynamic performance analysis.
  • Measured pickup by comparing bookings across multiple snapshots (if available).
  • Calculated KPIs: Room Nights, Revenue, Booking Volume, and ADR per lead time bucket.
  • Visualized key metrics in line and bar charts using Tableau for time-series insight.
  • Identified trends in booking behavior shifts pre-/post-season and across distribution types.
  • Highlighted early vs. late booking behavior differences between OTAs and Direct channels.

Booking Trends Analysis

ADR & RevPAR Weekly Variations

  • Trend Focus: Tracks how Average Daily Rate (ADR) changes week over week across different segments and seasons.
  • Year-over-Year Comparison: Identifies growth or decline in ADR between 2018, 2019, and 2020..
  • Market Sensitivity: Highlights segments most affected by pricing shifts and promotional strategies.
  • Stabilization or Volatility: Flags weeks with high volatility to help adjust future pricing strategies.
  • Data-Driven Pricing: Informs rate setting by identifying peak demand periods and price elasticity.
  • Seasonal Insights:: Reveals high-performing and underperforming weeks by ADR within each season..

Forecast vs Actual Performance

  • Forecast Accuracy: Compares projected room nights, revenue, and ADR against actual check-in data by week and segment..
  • Variance Analysis: Quantifies over- or under-performance using percentage deviations from forecasted values.
  • Segment-Level Insights: Highlights which segments most often exceed or fall short of expectations..
  • Lead Time Impact: Correlates forecast error with booking window and market behavior..
  • Continuous Calibration: Supports fine-tuning of forecasting models over time based on historical accuracy..
  • Operational Alignment: Informs revenue, staffing, and inventory decisions using reliable actuals vs projections comparison.
